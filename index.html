Intro

16%

Increase of Fatalities due to Road Accidents
2015-2016

Lets be honest, we know our roads are horrible. The death toll from 2015 to 2016 has risen from 144 to 168, that an increase of 16%!.

Let's make our roads safer. With DataSets from VicRoads, BOM and National Data Sources, using the latest technologies and past data we can predict based off current traffic and weather conditions, the likelyhood of an incident happening, anywhere.

We know that there are hotspots for these road deaths, what we can do is when matching situations (e.g. Rain Conditions, Road Coditions, etc.) why not alert nearby emergency services.

We can also fix these intersections and allow for a smoother travel home.

How it works

Using Google's TensorFlow, we feed in past data. Then when we open up our map, there will be two views, the citizen will use the application view, which tells them the hotspot rating of a coordinates where they tap. Whilst, Emergency Services Dispatch has the ability to view real-time alerts, so that when an incident occurs, they can dispatch the relevant services faster.
